Pancakes

Delicious fluffy pancakes topped with maple syrup and berries.

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 2 tbsp sugar
    • 2 tsp baking powder
  • Instructions:
    1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
    2. Add wet ingredients and mix until just combined.
    3. Cook on a hot griddle until bubbles form, then flip.

Omelette

A fluffy omelette filled with your favorite ingredients.

  • Ingredients:
    • 3 eggs
    • 2 tbsp milk
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
    2. Pour into a hot, greased skillet.
    3. Add fillings, fold, and cook until set.
