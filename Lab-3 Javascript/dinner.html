Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

  • Ingredients:
    • 200g spaghetti
    • 2 large eggs
    • 100g pancetta
  • Instructions:
    1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
    2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
    3. Whisk eggs and cheese, then add cooked spaghetti and pancetta.

Grilled Salmon

Healthy and delicious grilled salmon seasoned with herbs and lemon.

  • Ingredients:
    • 4 salmon fillets
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • Fresh herbs (e.g., dill, parsley)
  • Instructions:
    1. Preheat grill. Brush salmon with olive oil and season.
    2. Grill for 4-5 minutes per side.
    3. Serve with fresh herbs and lemon wedges.
